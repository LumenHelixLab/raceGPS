# Gameplay Design

## Physics Feel

Arcade street-racing: fast acceleration, forgiving steering, controlled drift, boost, dramatic camera, quick recovery, clean checkpoint timing.

## Cruise Mode

Social open room. Players can chat, drive, discover route objects, and signal nearby drivers.

## Race Mode

Straight race, route race, live lobby, private room, public matchmaking, ghost race, time trial.

## Challenge Mode

Object-based routes around tourist attractions, monuments, stadiums, parks, bridges, scenic drives, and natural features.

## Hot Pursuit

Role-based Cop vs Runner. Runner collects objects and escapes. Cop tags/intercepts within a validated radius.
